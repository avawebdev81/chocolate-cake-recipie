Chocolate Fudge Cake

Welcome to the ultimate guide for baking a rich and decadent chocolate fudge cake! This simple recipe yields a moist, chocolatey cake that is perfect for any occasion and is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • ​1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • ​3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • ​1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ​1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ​1 teaspoon salt
  • ​1 cup buttermilk or milk
  • ​1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • ​2 large eggs
  • ​1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ​1 cup hot water or coffee
  • ​Chocolate frosting (pre-made or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch pans
  2. ​Combine Dry: Whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Add Wet: Add buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Beat until combined.
  4. our Hot Liquid: Carefully stir in the hot water/coffee until the batter is smooth.
  5. Bake: Pour into prepared pans. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. ​Cool & Frost: Let cool completely on a rack before frosting and serving
